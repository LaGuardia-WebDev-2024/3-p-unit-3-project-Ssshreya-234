    • Recipe #1

    Ingredients Quantity
    Eggs 2
    Milk 1/4 cup
    Salt 1/8 teaspoon
    Slices of bread 2
    Vanilla Extract 1/8 teaspoon
    Ground of cinnomon 1 Pinch

    Step 1:Mix your eggs, milk, salt,vanilla extract, and cinnomon together into one bowl.

    Step 2:Put a slice of bread into the bowl and let it soak in the mixture for about a minute.

    Step 3:Next take your bread out of the bowl and put it onto a pan and let it cook completely on both sides.

    Step 4:Lastly, transfer your bread onto a plate and add any toppings you like! Enjoy!
